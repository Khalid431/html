Nelson Mandela

Former President of South Africa

About

Nelson Mandela was a South African anti-apartheid revolutionary, political leader, and philanthropist who served as President of South Africa from 1994 to 1999.

Achievements

  • Nobel Peace Prize Winner
  • First Black President of South Africa
  • Human Rights Activist

Timeline

  1. 1918 - Born in Mvezo, South Africa
  2. 1962 - Arrested and imprisoned
  3. 1990 - Released from prison
  4. 1994 - Became President of South Africa
  5. 2013 - Passed away

Famous Quote

"Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world."

Legacy

Nelson Mandela remains a global symbol of peace, equality, and freedom.
